# gameral_public

POCKET ARCADE PROJECT — (games 1–10 of 100)
Open index.html for the hub, or any game folder's index.html directly.
Pure HTML/CSS/JS, zero dependencies, works offline, touch + mouse, portrait-friendly.

01 breathe hold/release your bubble to fit each gate's band
02 halve cut the bar exactly in half; your error is trimmed away
03 polarity no steering — flip your magnetic charge to survive the field
04 lag a dodger whose controls arrive late, and the lag grows
05 wobble catch blocks while balancing the tower's centre of gravity
06 jarful herd fleeing fireflies into a jar; avoid the toad
07 crossroads you are the traffic light; four streams, zero crashes
08 mirror one tap jumps two runners — one lives on the ceiling
09 orbit you can only reverse direction; stars vs growing thorns
10 inkline one rationed ink stroke per drop; ramp the ball to the star
