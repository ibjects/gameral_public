World Module // Green Space

Park

When the terrain hash falls below 0.14, the brutalist concrete recedes to form a Park. Providing a visual breather for the endless city, these tiles lack the massive drop-shadows of buildings, anchoring them firmly to the ground layer.

The procedural engine scatters exactly five tree clusters per block. Each canopy utilizes a randomized radius and a precise +3px, +4px offset drop-shadow to emulate top-down volumetric depth without adding geometric complexity to the rendering loop.
