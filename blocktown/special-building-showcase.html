World Module // Landmarks

Special Building

When the AI Director mandates a landmark, standard mega-blocks are replaced by Special Buildings. These act as high-value properties, mission hubs, or narrative anchors.

Structurally, they adhere to the strict 260px grid and retain the standard yellow dashed road markings. However, the asphalt is painted a rich, dark gold. On the roof, glowing architectural holograms (icon art) represent world-famous monuments from a perfect top-down perspective, seamlessly blending with the game's UI.
