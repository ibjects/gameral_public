The AI Director

To experience the true game, you need a Google Gemini API key. This allows the engine to communicate directly with the AI to generate missions, shops, dialogue, and city layout in real-time.

  1. Go to Google AI Studio.
  2. Sign in and click Create API Key.
  3. Paste the key into the Blocktown main menu when starting a new Saga.

Security Note: Your API key is never stored on an external server. It is saved locally in your browser and sent directly to Google's servers.

The Vision: The Block Theory

Blocktown isn't just a map; it's a precisely defined mathematical grid designed to be the ultimate sandbox for Large Language Models.

Every tile in the game is exactly 260x260 pixels. The outer 45 pixels are strictly reserved for the asphalt and dashed yellow lines of the road matrix. This guarantees that no matter how the AI arranges the city, the streets always connect flawlessly.

But the real magic happens in the inner 170x170 pixel core. This is the seed area. It is a blank canvas where the AI Director can spawn procedurally generated skyscrapers, brutalist parking lots, lush parks, or custom narrative landmarks using raw HTML and SVG injections.

// The Future of Blocktown

Blocktown is an evolving experiment in AI-driven game design. Here is what we are exploring for future updates:

Community Snippet Market

Build custom HTML/SVG blocks in the Town Builder, save them as Snippets, and share them with the community. The AI Director will dynamically pull highly-rated community blocks into your live game.

Faction Wars & Territory

The AI will manage long-term territorial control between distinct gangs. Missions you accept will permanently alter neighborhood borders, gang colors, and street hostility levels.

Interiors & Layered Grids

Expanding the 170x170 canvas to support seamlessly walking into buildings. The AI will procedurally generate interior layouts, vaults, and safehouses on a secondary rendering layer.

Multi-Agent Director

Instead of a single AI prompt, splitting the workload. One lightweight model handles real-time traffic and weather moods, while a heavy reasoning model (like Gemini Pro) architects the grand storyline.
