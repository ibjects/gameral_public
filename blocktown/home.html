The Vision: The Block Theory

Blocktown isn't just a map; it's a precisely defined mathematical grid designed to be the ultimate sandbox for Large Language Models.

Every tile in the game is exactly 260x260 pixels. The outer 45 pixels are strictly reserved for the asphalt and dashed yellow lines of the road matrix. This guarantees that no matter how the AI arranges the city, the streets always connect flawlessly.

But the real magic happens in the inner 170x170 pixel core. This is the seed area. It is a blank canvas where the AI Director can spawn procedurally generated skyscrapers, brutalist parking lots, lush parks, or custom narrative landmarks. It knows exactly what fits, who can walk over it, and where the shadows fall.

Connecting the AI Director

To experience the "NEW SAGA (AI)" mode, you will need a Google Gemini API key. This allows the game engine to communicate directly with the AI model to generate missions, shops, dialogue, and city layout in real-time.

  • Head over to Google AI Studio.
  • Sign in and click Create API Key.
  • Copy the key and paste it into the Blocktown main menu when starting a new AI Saga.

Note: Your API key is never stored on a server. It is only saved locally in your browser's localStorage and sent directly to Google's servers during gameplay.
